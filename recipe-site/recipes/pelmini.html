Pelmini

Description

A nice pelmini. Basically like eastern european tortellini.

Ingredients

  • Some sort of pasta dough?
  • Meat filling
  • Luck
  • Courage

Steps

  1. Summon courage
  2. Roll dough into thin sheets and place on pelmini molds
  3. Put about a quarter dollop of meat on each pelmini spot
  4. Press second sheet on top to seal
